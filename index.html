Message from the Secretary General

Gandhi once said “Live as if you were to die tomorrow, Learn as if you were to live forever”.

In our lifetime, each one of us is trying to learn and emulate as much “Good” as we can. The only legacy that we leave behind is our deeds and the impact we make on lives of other people. In the modern world, Debate and Discussions are how we resolve conflicts. Diplomacy is no more an art but an efficient science by which we come to bilateral solutions.

So we, the students of NIT Rourkela have come up with the inception of the first edition of the NITR International Model United Nations Conference. It is said, “There was never a bad peace or A Good war”. But this peace comes at a price; peaceful dialogue among nations has averted so many wars and saved countless lives.

The power of the pen lies not in the amount of blood that it can spill but rather the number of lives that it can save. I am grateful to our respected Director Sir, our mentors, our sponsors and our partners for their trust, support and guidance that has motivated and inspired us to host the first ever NITR IMUN. We wish to embark a legacy that shall befit their trust in our abilities. I extend my gratitude to the Secretariat of NITR International MUN 2018, for their hard work and perseverance, ensuring an exemplary conference.

With this, I along with the entire Secretariat, look forward to hosting you this March at NITR International Model United Nations Conference, 2018.

Sincerely,

Mehul Anand

Secretary General

FAQs

Can NITRIMUNC provide a formal invitation to show in our college/university?

The NITRIMUNC is ready to provide invitation letters to persons/delegations who have registered,been approved and have paid their fees.

Is there a Dress code ?

Yes ,you have to put up a formal attire.

Is there any age limit/requirement for participating in NITRIMUNC?

There is no age requirement.

The accommodation will given inside the campus or outside?

Accommodation will be provided according to the choice of delegates. Accordingly the participation fee will vary.

How do I reach Rourkela by Air?

The nearest airports to Rourkela are that of Ranchi (225KM) and Bhubaneswar (341KM). Rourkela is well connected to these cities by Railways.

Can I get accommodation on 29th night?

Yes, you can avail the accommodation from 29th Night.

How can I check my allotments?

Allotted with any country, our Secretariat will call you as well as mail you the details of your allotments.

Money has been deducted from my bank account but receipt is not generated. What should I do?

In such cases kindly contact the NITR IMUNC Secretariat as soon as possible. Contact Mail id: secretariat@nitrimunc.org
